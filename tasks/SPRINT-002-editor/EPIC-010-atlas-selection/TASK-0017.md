# TASK-0017 — Implement Marquee Rectangle

## Summary

Implement the `MarqueeRectangle`, a reusable abstraction responsible for representing the geometric bounds of a marquee selection within Atlas Studio.

The Marquee Rectangle provides an immutable representation of the current drag-selection region. It normalizes pointer coordinates regardless of drag direction, enabling hit testing, selection filtering, viewport clipping, and future editor extensions.

The Marquee Rectangle is responsible only for describing the selection geometry. It does not render the rectangle, perform hit testing, modify documents, or execute business logic.

---

# Goal

Provide a reusable, extensible, and framework-independent Marquee Rectangle abstraction capable of representing drag-selection bounds through a stable public API.

---

# Background

Every marquee selection depends on a normalized rectangular region.

Typical examples include:

- Canvas drag selection
- Layer drag selection
- Timeline drag selection
- Asset browser drag selection
- Node editor selection
- Plugin-defined marquee selection

Instead of allowing each subsystem to calculate rectangle geometry independently, Atlas Studio should expose a unified Marquee Rectangle abstraction.

# Responsibilities

The Marquee Rectangle is responsible for:

- Representing drag-selection bounds.
- Normalizing rectangle coordinates.
- Providing immutable geometry metadata.
- Supporting future geometric extensions.
- Serving as the canonical marquee geometry.

The Marquee Rectangle is **not** responsible for:

- Rendering.
- Hit testing.
- Updating selections.
- Executing commands.
- Business logic.
